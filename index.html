As a researcher I have intensively worked on:

Visual navigation and SLAM Monocular SLAM systems Autonomous Aerial Vehicles
3D stereo reconstruction Visit Research page to read more.

My recent work has strengthened my belief towards artificial intelligence and its application in the field of robotics, I believe that some of the very challenging problems in robotics can be tackled through machine intelligence. My current research interests revolve around deep reinforcement learning, cloth manipulation through human demonstrations and human-robot interaction.
